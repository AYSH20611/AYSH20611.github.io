Exhibits

Art lovers are in for a treat this summer as there are loads of exhibitions carrying on throughout the season.

Digital Art

@digitalart

The Van Gogh X Japan exhibition is a digital event that explores the relationship between the Japanese Ukiyo-e style and Van Gogh's paintings. A story told from the 360-degree perspective, the walk-through gallery exhibition is running from Friday July 14 to Monday July 31, from 10am to 10pm.

Isthmus

@isthmus

An exhibition curated by Sophie Mayuko Arni, Isthmus is a collection of photographs showcasing the human experience through design choice. The images depict furniture, objects, wall trimmings and windows to display socio-economic and culture over the decades. Shots include images of the Queen Elizabeth II.
